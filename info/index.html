a bit about me

Welcome!#

      I’m Harsha - I go to Duke, where I study math and computer science. Among many things, I’m interested in healthcare and understanding how people think (and how to optimize it). One of my dreams is to build a firm designed for everyday people like you and me, that can democratize inaccessible and uninterpretable tools to complement our everyday decision-making.

      I’m a fan of heuristics, and often rely on its simplicity to make sense of disorder and unpredictability. I like to think that my life experiences molded me into a generalist - a wannabe diplomat trying to recognize interdisciplinary ideas and perspectives in the pursuit of a more holistic appreciation of reality.

      I really love moving people through storytelling (despite my outwardly quiet and stoic demeanor). I actually think I’d be a performer in another world where money wasn’t a factor (most people wouldn’t pay to watch that). I also enjoy music - it’s a great way to build experiential memories. Chess and basketball are also very fun.

      I’ve worked on and am working on some interesting projects during my time at Duke. Take a look if you feel so inclined.

      If you’re intrigued, get in touch - I love meeting new people.
